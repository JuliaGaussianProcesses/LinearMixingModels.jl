LinearMixingModels.ILMM — Type
ILMM(fs, H, D)

An Instantaneous Linear Mixing Model (ILMM) – a distribution over vector- valued functions. Let p be the number of observed outputs, and m the number of latent processes, then

Arguments:

  • fs: a length-m vector of Gaussian process objects from AbstractGPs.jl.
  • H: a p x m matrix representing a fixed basis of our p-dim target: h1,...,hm
  • D: an m x m Diagonal matrix, variance of noise on each latent process. Positive entries.

LinearMixingModels.OILMM — Type
OILMM(fs, H, D)

An Orthogonal Instantaneous Linear Mixing Model (OILMM) – a distribution over vector- valued functions. Let p be the number of observed outputs, and m the number of latent processes, then

Arguments:

  • fs: a length-m vector of Gaussian process objects as an IndependentMOGP.
  • H: a p x m orthogonal matrix representing a fixed basis of our p-dim target: h1,...,hm
  • D: an m x m Diagonal matrix, variance of noise on each latent process. Positive entries.
